Who's HandUp for?


Female graduates or career changers aspiring to work in technology.


Looking to start or progress your career as a developer, designer or product manager?

Get a Hand Up with a professional mentor to guide your next steps.

Why do we need it?

Women are still vastly under-represented in the tech workforce, making it more difficult to find the right professional support and peers to connect and learn with.

Not only is this a problem for young women themselves, but un-diverse teams will lead to bias in products and the tech future we will live in.

The solution

A platform to help women match with mentors online and document their journey for accountability.

Aspiring female technologists can also connect with peers to form local learning or hackathon teams.
